Project #3: Documentary

Caution

Crime scenes. Crash sites. Construction zones. These are the places where we would expect to see caution tapes, the visual sign that tells us to stay away.

However, due to COVID-19, we now see it at places such as public playgrounds and parks, places where children can have fun, and that are not commonly associated with the image of caution tapes.
