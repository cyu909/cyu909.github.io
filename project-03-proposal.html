Project #3: Documentary

The Proposal

For this project I will be exploring the impact of COVID-19 by shooting the playgrounds in the park. Due to COVID-19, the childrenâ€™s playgrounds and facilities are subject to closure and are surrounded by yellow caution tapes. I found this contrast really interesting when visiting the parks, because generally we would not expect to see caution tapes in the playgrounds where children can play and have fun.

For more information, I will read related news articles, then find different local parks and read the warning signs at the public parks. I will look for the equipment that is wrapped by the caution tapes, and photograph these scenes. As for the image making approaches, I am considering editing the photos by selectively applying black and white effect to emphasize the subject.
